# Apoxer Brand Identity Guide
## Logo Generation Brief for AI Image Generation

---

## Brand Overview

**Apoxer** is a modern gaming matchmaking platform that connects players across 50,000+ games. The name "Apoxer" suggests proximity, connection, and bringing players together ("apo" = near/proximity, "xer" = modern tech suffix).

**Mission**: To make finding players effortless. Gaming is better when shared, and Apoxer helps people connect faster, team up smarter, and enjoy multiplayer without friction.

**Tagline**: "Find your squad, instantly."

---

## Brand Personality

- **Modern & Tech-Forward**: Cutting-edge technology, real-time features, fast performance
- **Gaming-Focused**: Built by gamers, for gamers
- **Community-Driven**: Bringing players together, building connections
- **Bold & Confident**: Strong, assertive, action-oriented
- **Clean & Minimal**: No unnecessary elements, focused on functionality
- **Inclusive**: All platforms, all games, all players welcome

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Cyan (Primary Accent)**
- Hex: `#06b6d4`
- RGB: `rgb(6, 182, 212)`
- Usage: Primary CTA buttons, highlights, active states, brand elements
- Meaning: Energy, technology, connection, digital gaming

**Dark Slate (Background)**
- Hex: `#0f172a` (slate-900)
- RGB: `rgb(15, 23, 42)`
- Usage: Main background, dark theme base
- Meaning: Professional, modern, gaming-focused

**Medium Slate (Secondary Background)**
- Hex: `#1e293b` (slate-800)
- RGB: `rgb(30, 41, 59)`
- Usage: Cards, elevated surfaces, secondary backgrounds

**Light Slate (Text/UI)**
- Hex: `#334155` (slate-700)
- RGB: `rgb(51, 65, 85)`
- Usage: Borders, dividers, subtle UI elements

### Secondary Colors

**White/Off-White (Text)**
- Hex: `#e2e8f0` (slate-200)
- RGB: `rgb(226, 232, 240)`
- Usage: Primary text, high contrast elements

**Green Accent (Optional)**
- Hex: `#75ef0b` (app-green-500)
- RGB: `rgb(117, 239, 11)`
- Usage: Success states, special highlights (used sparingly)

**Yellow Accent (Optional)**
- Hex: `#fbbf24` (amber-400)
- RGB: `rgb(251, 191, 36)`
- Usage: Warnings, special highlights (used sparingly)

### Color Usage Guidelines

- **Primary**: Cyan should be the dominant brand color in the logo
- **Background**: Dark slate for contrast and gaming aesthetic
- **Accent**: Use green/yellow sparingly, primarily for special states
- **Contrast**: Ensure high contrast for readability and accessibility

## Visual Style & Aesthetics

### Design Principles

1. **Angular & Geometric**
   - No rounded corners (border-radius: 0)
   - Sharp, clean edges
   - Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
   - Gaming-inspired angularity

2. **Dark Theme**
   - Deep slate backgrounds
   - High contrast with cyan accents
   - Optimized for extended viewing (gaming sessions)

3. **Minimal & Clean**
   - No unnecessary decorative elements
   - Focus on functionality
   - Clear visual hierarchy
   - Space is important

4. **Bold & Confident**
   - Strong, assertive shapes
   - High contrast
   - Commanding presence
   - Action-oriented

5. **Tech & Gaming**
   - Digital, pixel-perfect precision
   - Modern gaming aesthetic
   - Connection/networking themes
   - Real-time, dynamic feel

### Visual Elements

- **Corner Brackets**: Angular corner brackets used throughout UI (gaming aesthetic)
- **Sharp Edges**: All elements have sharp, clean edges
- **Geometric Shapes**: Squares, rectangles, angular forms
- **Gradient Accents**: Subtle cyan gradients for depth
- **Glass Effects**: Subtle backdrop blur for modern UI elements

## Logo Requirements & Specifications

### Logo Concept Ideas

1. **Connection/Network Theme**
   - Nodes connecting (representing players connecting)
   - Network/graph visualization
   - Link/chain elements
   - Convergence of paths

2. **Gaming/Controller Elements**
   - Abstracted controller shapes
   - Gamepad-inspired geometry
   - Angular, modern interpretation
   - NOT literal controller (too generic)

3. **Letter-Based (Monogram)**
   - "A" or "AP" monogram
   - Angular, geometric letterforms
   - Modern, tech-forward
   - Could incorporate connection/networking elements

4. **Abstract Symbol**
   - Geometric shape representing connection
   - Angular, sharp forms
   - Could suggest proximity/convergence
   - Unique, memorable mark

### Logo Specifications

**Style Requirements:**
- **Angular & Geometric**: Sharp edges, no rounded corners
- **Modern & Tech-Forward**: Contemporary, digital aesthetic
- **Bold & Confident**: Strong presence, high contrast
- **Gaming-Inspired**: Should feel at home in gaming context
- **Minimal**: Clean, uncluttered, focused

**Color Requirements:**
- **Primary**: Cyan (#06b6d4) as main color
- **Background**: Dark slate (#0f172a) for contrast
- **Optional**: White/light for light backgrounds
- **Monochrome**: Should work in single color (cyan or white)

**Technical Requirements:**
- **Scalable**: Works from favicon (16x16) to billboard size
- **Versatile**: Horizontal and vertical/stacked versions
- **Readable**: Clear at small sizes
- **Distinctive**: Unique, memorable, not generic

**Logo Variations Needed:**
1. **Full Logo**: Wordmark + symbol (horizontal)
2. **Icon/Symbol**: Standalone mark (square format)
3. **Wordmark**: Text-only version
4. **Monochrome**: Single color versions (cyan, white, dark)
5. **Favicon**: Simplified version for small sizes
