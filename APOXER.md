## About Apoxer

Apoxer is a modern gaming matchmaking platform designed to help gamers find teammates, join active lobbies, and build gaming communities. Whether you're looking for a quick match or want to organize regular gaming sessions, Apoxer connects you with players who share your gaming interests and playstyle.

Our platform supports **50,000+ games** and provides fast matchmaking, real-time lobby management, and community-driven events to bring gamers together.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Click "Sign In" in the top right corner and create an account using email authentication. It's free and takes less than a minute.

**Q: Is Apoxer free to use?**  
A: Yes! Apoxer is completely free to use. You can search games, join lobbies, create lobbies, follow players, and participate in community events all for free. Premium features are optional and provide additional convenience and community-building tools.

**Q: What platforms does Apoxer support?**  
A: Apoxer supports all major gaming platforms including PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, Mobile, and Other. You can filter lobbies and events by platform.

### Lobbies

**Q: How many lobbies can I be in at once?**  
A: You can only be in one active lobby at a time (either hosting or as a member). If you try to join a new lobby, you'll automatically leave your current one.

**Q: What happens if I leave a lobby?**  
A: When you leave a lobby, you'll be removed from the member list and can join or create a new lobby. If you were the host, the lobby will close.

**Q: How do I invite players to my lobby?**  
A: You can invite players by:
- Using the "Invite to Lobby" button on their profile
- Using the auto-invite feature (Pro users)
- Sharing your lobby link directly

**Q: Can I kick or ban players from my lobby?**  
A: Yes, if you're the host, you can kick or ban players from your lobby using the member management controls.

### Events

**Q: How do weekly community events work?**  
A: Each week, the community votes on games they want to play together. The top-voted games become 6-hour events where players can join and participate. You can vote for multiple games and choose your preferred time slots.

**Q: Can I create my own events?**  
A: Yes, Pro users can create and manage their own events. Free users can participate in all events but cannot create them.

**Q: How are event times determined?**  
A: Event times are determined by the most popular time preferences from voters. The system analyzes all votes and selects the time slot with the most support.

### Premium

**Q: What's included in the Pro plan?**  
A: Pro includes auto-invite system, event creation and management, custom profile banners, Pro badge, and upcoming collections feature. See the [Billing page](/billing) for full details.

**Q: Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?**  
A: Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your billing period.

**Q: How do I upgrade to Pro?**  
A: Visit the [Billing page](/billing) and click "Upgrade to Pro". You'll be redirected to a secure checkout process powered by Stripe.

### Technical

**Q: Does Apoxer work on mobile?**  
A: Yes! Apoxer is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. You can access all features from any device with a web browser.

**Q: How does real-time chat work?**  
A: Apoxer uses Supabase Realtime to provide instant messaging in lobbies. Messages appear instantly for all members without page refreshes.

**Q: What games are supported?**  
A: Apoxer supports over 50,000 games from SteamGridDB's database. If a game exists on SteamGridDB, you can search for it and create lobbies for it.

### Privacy & Safety

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes, we use industry-standard security practices and Supabase's secure infrastructure. Your password is encrypted and we never store sensitive payment information (handled by Stripe).

**Q: How do I report a user?**  
A: Visit the user's profile page and click the "Report User" button. You can select a reason and provide additional details.

**Q: Can I make my profile private?**  
A: Yes, you can set your profile to private in your profile settings. Private profiles are only visible to users you follow or who follow you.
